MUFFINNNNSSS!

Step 1 Preheat oven to 350 degrees F (175 degrees C). Spray 12 muffin cups with cooking spray. Step 2 Mix the flour, baking powder, baking soda, and salt in a bowl. Step 3 In another bowl, stir together the butter, egg, milk, zucchini, and garlic until well blended. Mix the flour mixture into the milk mixture, about 1/2 cup at a time, stirring between additions, until the flour mixture is incorporated. Fold in the Cheddar cheese, Parmesan cheese, and crumbled bacon, and pour the batter into the prepared muffin cups. Step 4 Bake in the preheated oven until a toothpick inserted into the center of a muffin comes out clean, 30 to 35 minutes. Allow muffins to cool slightly before removing from muffin cups; serve warm. Refrigerate leftovers.
